Why?

April 20, 2025


Q: Why?
A: Why not?

Q: People won't read your blog because their attention spans have become
   shorter due to social media.
A: Well, the truth is beyond these four alternatives listed below.
   1. This is for others to read. (x)
   2. This is not for others to read. (~x)
   3. This is both for others to read and not for others to read. (x ∨ ~x)
   4. This is neither for others to read nor for not others to read. ~(x ∨ ~x)

Q: What will you write about?
A: Everything that can be represented in symbols.

Q: What is the purpose of this blog?
A: To explore the fragile nature of existence of things or is it?

Q: What will your next blog post be about?
A: Why do you want to know? Can we not have "I do not know" as a valid answer?
